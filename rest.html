Too busy to cook but tired of takeaway? Have a special event that you would like catered? On a restricted diet or just looking to try different foods? With a single tap, your mobile device becomes your personal chef menu. Introducing Chefly, an app that puts you in touch with local cooks eager to prepare fresh, delicious and affordable meals for you! Here’s how it works: Choose your favourite kind of food (for example, Spanish tapas, Italian pasta, organic, seafood) and Chefly quickly locates nearby personal chefs and home cooks that match your preferences. Browse through their profiles, consult reviews and then contact them directly. It’s that simple! Ideal for people of all ages and budgets, Chefly saves you time and lets you enjoy great homemade meals cooked to your specifications whether for a special occasion or on a regular basis.

You can become somebody's favourite chef. If you are already a personal chef or just enjoy cooking for family and friends, why not earn some extra money in the process? Sign up as chef on Chefly. You control your specialty, indicate which kinds of cooking services you offer (e.g., catering for special events, daily dinners and/or individual meals), set your prices and decide which orders to accept. On Chefly you earn money with each completed order and your clients will rate and review your cooking and customer service. Positive feedback often translates into more opportunities for additional clients and more revenue!
