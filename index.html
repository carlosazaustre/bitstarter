Frequenly Asked Questions

How Chefly works?

Chefly has two kind of user. The “Client”, who looks for chefs in the app in order to save time. You can choose your favorite foods, if you would like use your own ingredients or your chef’s ingredients, if you prefer the chef cooks in your house or he brings the meal done.
The other user is the “Chef”. you offer your services and choose your price. You can make the meal in your home or in the client’s house. You can buy the ingredients or to use the ingredients bought by the client.

How Chefly costs?

The app is free. You only must pay the chef once he makes your meal. If you are a chef, Chefly only charges a percent for each task you done.

Can I sign up as Chef?

Of course! We encourage you done and you start to get good reviews in order to get new tasks and more and more. You put your price, your preferences and your location. Chefly does the rest.

If I'm Chef, When I receive the money?

Chefly is based on a recommendations and reviews systems. When you finish a meal task, the client will pay you the agreed price and he rating and review you. At the end of the month, Chefly will count your finished tasks and will charge you a percentage (8%) for services. If you have not taks in a month, you will not be charged.

Where Chefly is active?

Chefly works all over the world. The app shows the chefs nearby to your location. If you don’t see chefs in your area, you can spread the word!

When Chefly will be launched?

Chefly will be launched very soon! For the time being, it is a crowdfunding campaign in order to determine the feasibility of the project.
