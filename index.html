What is Chefly?

Too busy to cook but tired of takeaway? Have a special event that you would like catered? On a restricted diet or just looking to try different foods? With a single tap, your mobile device becomes your personal chef menu.

Introducing Chefly, an app that puts you in touch with local cooks eager to prepare fresh, delicious and affordable meals for you!

Homemade food

Choose your favourite kind of food (for example, Spanish tapas, Italian pasta, organic, seafood...)

Location

Chefly quickly locates nearby personal chefs and home cooks that match your preferences.

Save Time

Chefly save your time and lets you enjoy great homemade meals cooked to your specifications whether for a special occasion or on a regular basis.

Get Reviews

If you are a Chef user, your clients will rate and review your cooking and customer service.

Frequenly Asked Questions

How Chefly works?

Chefly has two kind of users. The “Client”, who looks for chefs who cook for him/her for a specific event or in a ordinary way. You can choose your favorite foods, using your own ingredients or your chef’s ingredients, the location where the meal should be prepared (your home or chef's home etc).
The other user is the “Chef”. you offer your services and choose your price and the area where you are going to cook. You can make the meal in your own home or in the client’s house. You can buy the ingredients or use the ingredients bought by the client.

Can I sign up as Chef?

Of course! We encourage you to start right now and get more and more reviews and new clients! You put your price, your preferences and your location. Chefly does the rest.

Where is Chefly active?

Chefly works all over the world. The app shows the chefs nearby to your location. If you don’t see chefs in your area, you can spread the word!

When will be Chefly launched?

Chefly will be launched very soon! For the time being, it is a crowdfunding campaign in order to determine the feasibility of the project.
